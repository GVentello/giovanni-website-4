    Giovanni Ventello is a musician, producer, engineer and singer songwriter from Lawrence, Kansas now based in New Orleans. Giovanni, although having the most experience working on folk, indie, rock and punk records, works tirelessly to expand his musical resume working with talented artists in any and all genres. He is currently working on the upcoming Aniya Teno upcoming single, “If You Do” with Wolf Moon Records, as well as the debut self-titled Coal album. Giovanni began working in recording in Lawrence, Kansas as a freelance engineer. Recording grungy local acts in attics and basements. In 2019 he began working with LAAND (Lawrence All Ages Noise Destination) productioning company, producing live sessions with artists: Chess Club, LK Ultra, Matt Pryor (of The Get Up Kids) and many more. In 2021 Giovanni moved to New Orleans, Louisiana where he began studying recording at Loyola University and working at the Maple Leaf Bar as a live sound engineer. In addition to the Maple Leaf and college classes Giovanni produces and engineers for many local acts including Aniya Teno, Kevin Gullage, Lou Hill and others. In addition to production Giovanni is a performing and recording artist. Giovanni writes lyric driven indie folk with a hint of the Kansas blue grass that he was raised on. His songs are personal but remain accessible and relatable to most who hear them. Whether performing solo or with a stirring rock band his performances are destined to resonate with those present. Giovanni’s music serves to inform his production work and vice versa.
