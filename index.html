    Giovanni Ventello is a musician, producer, engineer and singer songwriter from Lawrence, Kansas now based in New Orleans. Giovanni, although having the most experience working on folk, indie, rock and punk records, works tirelessly to expand his musical resume working with talented artists in any and all genres. He is currently working on the upcoming Aniya Teno upcoming single, “If You Do” with Wolf Moon Records, as well as the debut self-titled Coal album.
